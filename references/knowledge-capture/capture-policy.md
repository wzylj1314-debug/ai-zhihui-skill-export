# Knowledge Capture Policy

Use this policy with `zhihui-knowledge-capture-decision`.

## Store

Store content when it is:

- Reusable across multiple agents or support cases.
- Clear enough to be turned into a stable answer, rule, or classification example.
- Supported by real customer wording or repeated operational need.
- Safe after removing private customer, account, token, contract, and screenshot data.
- Not already covered by an existing reference.

## Temporarily Hold

Temporarily hold content when it may be useful but needs:

- More examples.
- Product owner confirmation.
- Customer-service lead confirmation.
- Commercial, legal, privacy, or capability review.

## Do Not Store

Do not store content when it is:

- One-off noise.
- Too vague to support future judgment.
- Duplicated by existing FAQ or rules.
- Mainly raw personal/customer data.
- A temporary product bug without stable troubleshooting value.
- A business promise that has not been approved.

## Manual Review Required

Manual review is required for:

- Price, discount, package, invoice, refund, or contract wording.
- Copyright, commercial use, legal liability, or data privacy.
- API, private deployment, custom integration, or enterprise promise.
- Complaints, repeated failures, negative customer sentiment, or account disputes.
- Any draft that could be sent externally as official wording.
